Can we be really who we claimed

Sichem Zhou | Sat 26 May 2018

Five months ago, when I just failed my first French test, I wrote the last journal, with full of my emotions and sorrow. c'était l'hiver, the freezing temperature and heavy snow bring the depressions. Now the two are gone, at least for the quit a few months, and the depression stayed. Some weeks are better and other weeks are worse, if I have to say, the black dog has been chasing me, better days when I focused on something else, worse days come when there is too much me living inside me, THIS SUCKS.

Life never went what I thought it to be, but when I was younger, there seems always hopes, possibilities churned up the reality, I turned to brag to myself and my poor memory only reminds me when it was too late. Well, Since I was the guy who caused it, forgiveness was given easily. All those images seemed inches out of my finger, learning the new nature/programming language, learn music, midis, refine my computational geometry knowledge, being more socialized and likable. I claimed these are easy, well, now I think of it, the reality teaches me "watching Netflix with beer and wasting time on cellphones" are the real easy things, what a disappointment of reality.

The image of the slightly better version of myself, it seems that he can live a much better life and pursuit the success. It is feasible, he really requires not superpowers or being rich as Bruce Wayne. But yet, this super-version of myself is 100 steps ahead of me everyday. Well, in details, I guess he would not feel sleepy anytime during the day, has a urge to eat something from time to time or open YouTube pages from time to times when stuck in thought or bugs. He would somehow have energy and find the time to keep studying computational photography, graphics algorithm or even the music theories which I promised myself. Are all the inertia destroying my life, leading me to an unpleasant, boring life? But it does scare me. It reminds me of the depressed version of Peter Parker, never became Spider-Man, never was able to get Mary Jane. is in reality that most of us are that boring Peter Parker?

If only I can keep this mentality forever. Unfortunately on Mondays, Tuesdays, some voices in my head would start whispering again. "Come on, you can start watching Netflix, forget about all the tasks, and things you want to do. You can always do it tomorrow, Just waste your time on Netflix for next 2 hours, the dopamine will be happy."

Well, no answers.
